How It Works

Placing an order at Sweet Cafe is simple:

  1. Fill out your personal and order details in the form.
  2. Select whether you'd like to pick up your order or have it delivered.
  3. If choosing delivery, provide your delivery address.
  4. Submit the form, and we'll confirm your order via email or phone!
