How Can We Assist You?

Welcome to the Help Center! Whether you need assistance with placing an order, have questions about our menu, or need customer service support, we're here to help.

How to Place an Order

Placing an order with Sweet Cafe is easy! You can choose from the following options:

  • Online Orders: Visit our Order Now page, select your items, and checkout securely.
  • In-Person Orders: Drop by our cafe, browse the menu, and place your order at the counter.
  • Phone Orders: Call us at (123) 456-7890 and we'll prepare your order for pickup.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What are your cafe's hours?

We are open every day from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM. We look forward to serving you!

Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes! We offer a variety of vegan and gluten-free options. Check our menu for more details.

Do you offer delivery?

At this moment, we do not offer delivery directly, but we are available on several delivery platforms like Uber Eats and DoorDash!

Can I customize my order?

Absolutely! Whether you want a different milk, extra shots of espresso, or special requests, feel free to customize your order when you place it.
